Challenge 08 - Basic scripting

Goal: Write a quick script or one-liner to extract the token from logs.txt.

Hints:
- The token appears once as TOKEN=... in /challenge/logs.txt

Solve (example):
python3 - <<'PY'
import re
text = open('/challenge/logs.txt').read()
print(re.search(r'TOKEN=(\S+)', text).group(1))
PY
